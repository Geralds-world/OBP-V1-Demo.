Onderstepoort Biological Products SOC LTD


Innovators in the Development and Supply of Animal Bio-Pharmaceuticals


OBP is South Africa's leading distributor of livestock vaccines. We serve individuals, farming communities and distribution partners locally, on the African continent, and internationally.







About Us

Solution - Driven


Since our inception in 2000, our primary objective has remained steadfast and clear- we introduce the newest manufacturing technologies to develop our products.

Technology - Driven


We have evolved through a rich past defined by world firsts. Today, OBP embraces technology enhanced by digital solutions that answer modern animal health challenges.

Service - Driven


Supported by a strong foundation merging modern expertise with engaged partnerships, OBP is committed to making the difference in key areas affecting our local and global communities.

THE PROCUREMENT PROCESS AT OBP

OUR CURRENT PROCUREMENT NEEDS

IDENTIFY YOUR BUSINESS SUITABILITY

Qualify your business profile, financial capabilities and ability to deliver against all requirements listed on your RFQ of choice.
